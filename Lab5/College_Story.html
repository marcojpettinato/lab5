Background

Once I left high school to play basketball in college things became increasingly difficult. The biggest change is the time management and things that are asked of you as a college athlete. Ones my priorities are challenge day in and day out. You are asked to ballance things like:

  1. Basketball

  2. School

  3. Family

  4. Friends

After some time I learned how to balance these things and being a college athlete became one of the most fullfilling and funs things I have ever done in my life.
